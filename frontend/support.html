Common Questions

Quick answers to the most frequent queries we receive.

How do I list my PG on Nestify?
Sign up as an owner, go to your dashboard, and click "Add New PG". Fill in your property details, upload photos, and hit Publish. Your listing goes live within minutes after review.
Is Nestify free for tenants?
Yes! Searching, shortlisting, and contacting PG owners is completely free for tenants. We charge a small commission only to property owners when a booking is confirmed.
How are listings verified?
Our team reviews every listing before it goes live. Owners are required to submit identity proof and property documents. Listings with the "Verified" badge have been physically inspected.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email. You'll receive a reset link valid for 30 minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.
Can I edit my support request after submitting?
Once submitted, requests can't be edited. You can always send a follow-up ticket with additional details — our team links related requests automatically.
