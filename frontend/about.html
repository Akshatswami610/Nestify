Our Story

We lived the problem. So we built the solution.

Finding a good PG near college or workplace has always meant endless phone calls, blurry WhatsApp photos, and owners who ghost you after you've already paid the token amount. We've been there.

Nestify was born out of that frustration. We built a platform where every listing is verified, every owner is accountable, and every student or professional can find a place they're actually excited to live in — not just settle for.
