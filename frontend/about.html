About Nestify

Your home away from home — without the guesswork.

Our Story

Finding a good PG near college or workplace has always meant endless phone calls, blurry WhatsApp photos, and owners who ghost you after you've already paid the token amount. We've been there.

Nestify was born out of that frustration. We built a platform where every listing is verified, every owner is accountable, and every student or professional can find a place they're actually excited to live in — not just settle for.

What We Offer

  • ✔ Verified PG listings with real photos
  • ✔ Search by location, budget & amenities
  • ✔ Direct booking requests — no middlemen
  • ✔ Owner dashboard for managing enquiries

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves a safe, comfortable place to call home while they chase their goals. Our mission is to remove the friction, the mistrust, and the stress from finding a PG — so you can focus on what actually matters.
