We believe in plain language. This policy explains exactly what data Nestify collects, why we collect it, and how you can control it. If anything is unclear, reach out — we're happy to explain.

Section 01

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated automatically when you use Nestify, and in some cases, information from third parties.

Information you provide:

  • Account registration details — name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Profile information — city, role (student / working professional / PG owner), and preferences.
  • Listing details provided by PG owners — address, amenities, photos, pricing, and availability.
  • Booking enquiries and messages sent through our platform.
  • Feedback, reviews, and support communications.

Information collected automatically:

  • Device and browser information — IP address, browser type, operating system.
  • Usage data — pages visited, search queries, filters applied, time spent.
  • Location data — approximate city-level location, if permitted by your device.
  • Log data — server logs, error reports, and performance metrics.
Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, improve, and personalise Nestify. Specifically:

  1. To create and manage your account, and authenticate your identity securely.
  2. To match renters with relevant PG listings based on location, budget, and preferences.
  3. To facilitate communication between renters and PG owners through our platform.
  4. To verify PG listings and maintain the quality and accuracy of our database.
  5. To send transactional notifications — booking confirmations, enquiry updates, account alerts.
  6. To improve our search algorithms, recommendation engine, and overall product experience.
  7. To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and other harmful activity.
  8. To comply with legal obligations under applicable Indian law.
📧

We may send you occasional product updates or feature announcements. You can opt out of these at any time from your account settings or by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email.

Section 03

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With PG owners: When you submit a booking enquiry, your name and contact details are shared with the relevant property owner to facilitate the booking.
  • With service providers: Trusted third-party vendors who help us operate Nestify — including cloud hosting, email delivery, and analytics — under strict data processing agreements.
  • For legal compliance: When required by law, court order, or government authority under Indian law.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, with appropriate notice to you.
🔒

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers or data brokers. Nestify does not display third-party advertisements.

Section 04

Cookies & Tracking

Nestify uses cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our platform.

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function — authentication sessions, security tokens, and form state. Cannot be disabled.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your search filters, location preferences, and display settings.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand page performance and how features are used — no personally identifiable information is tracked.

You can manage or disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain platform features.

Section 05

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS using TLS encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store them in plain text.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel only.
  • We conduct periodic security reviews and vulnerability assessments.
  • Authentication tokens are short-lived and rotated regularly.

While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is completely immune to security risks. If you suspect your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

Section 06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data on Nestify:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile at any time.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to our retention obligations.
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing, such as marketing communications.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 11. We will respond within 30 days.

Section 07

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your account and deleted within 90 days of account closure, unless required by law.
  • Booking and transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and accounting compliance.
  • Anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked to any individual.
  • Support communications are retained for up to 2 years.
Section 08

Third-Party Links

Nestify may contain links to external websites or services not operated by us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or data handling of third-party websites linked from our platform.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

Nestify is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Section 10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Send an email notification to registered users for significant changes.
  • Display a notice on the platform for a reasonable period after the update.

Your continued use of Nestify after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
