About Nestify

Making PG hunting simple, transparent, and stress-free.

Our Story

Finding a good PG near college or workplace is often confusing and time-consuming. Nestify was built to simplify that process by connecting students directly with PG owners.

Our goal is to create a platform where users can easily explore, compare, and book visits without unnecessary hassle.

What We Offer

  • ✔ Verified PG listings
  • ✔ Easy search & filters
  • ✔ Direct booking requests
  • ✔ Owner dashboard for management

Our Mission

To make accommodation discovery seamless and reliable for students and working professionals by leveraging modern technology and user-friendly design.
